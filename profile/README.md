## Brand Guidelines

All deliverables from this org follow the **[Keck Medicine of USC Brand & Design Guidelines](https://github.com/Keck-Marketing-and-Analytics/.github/blob/main/docs/Keck-Brand-Template.md)**.

Key standards at a glance:
- **Primary color:** Keck Red `#9B0000`
- **Accent:** Gold `#F5C518`, Olive Green `#7A8B2E`
- **Headlines:** Georgia — **Body:** Calibri
- **Background:** Off-white `#F7F6F2`, white cards
